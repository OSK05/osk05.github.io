About

The DJ Behind the Decks

I'm DJ O.S.K, a UK based, open format DJ performing for private events and club nights. I show up prepared, read the crowd, and adapt to client needs from the point of booking to the end of my performance.

With thousands of hours experience in professional settings, I draw on a wide range of techniques to suit each event.

I focus on doing the job properly --- performing well, reading the crowd and delivering on my client's expectations. This is proven by my reviews and my vast experience over the last 2 years.
